Tokaji Furmint - Carpinus

Short Description

Some description of the wine

Molly's Rating and Tasting Notes

7.0. I thought it smelled like green apple. It was nicer than I thought a dry white would be for me, since this isn't normally what I go for.

Ryan's Tasting Notes

7.5. Not sure if the glass, but I was getting musky (like an old bookstore) on the nose. Pretty tart, similar to the sensation of a not-so-ripe raspeberry, but tastes of green apple and maybe hints of honey. My first sip had a taste of pepper, but I can no longer pick that up, as I have gotten more intoxicated. There is also an undertone of a darker fruit (maybe a stone fuit) lurking underneath the tart brightness that tantalizes the mouth on first exposure.

Additional Information

Purchased at Wall-to-Wall Wine in Lincoln, NE. It was about $15, which is probably what you should expect to pay for this wine. (? should check that)
